About StarRush

StarRush Trends Analysis is an advanced tool designed to collect and analyze trending information from platforms like Pinterest and Google Trends. Our goal is to reveal insights into what people are searching for online and help businesses or individuals stay ahead of the curve.

Note: This application is currently under development and awaiting approval for API access from Pinterest. Once approved, we will integrate Pinterest's API to enhance our data collection and analysis capabilities.
